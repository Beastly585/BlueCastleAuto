What We Do

Our Services

Full-service shop handling all domestic and most import vehicles. We diagnose accurately, explain everything in plain terms, and fix it right the first time.

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, spark plugs, filters, belts, and fluid services. Conventional and synthetic oil using the spec for your vehicle. We follow your manufacturer schedule so nothing gets missed.

Request this service →

Alignment & Brakes

Front-end and four-wheel alignment starting at $69.99. Pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines. We inspect the full system and only recommend what actually needs replacing.

Request this service →

Steering & Suspension

Shocks, struts, tie rods, ball joints, and control arms. If your car pulls, floats, or clunks over bumps, we find the issue and give you a straight answer on what it takes to fix it.

Request this service →

Electrical

Battery, alternator, starter, and wiring. We use proper diagnostic tools to trace electrical faults accurately — no guesswork, no unnecessary parts.

Request this service →

Radiators & Cooling

Radiator repair and replacement, coolant flush, hoses, thermostat, and water pump. AC diagnosis, recharge, and compressor repair. Albuquerque summers are no joke — we keep you cool.

Request this service →

Diagnostics & Tune-Ups

Check engine light, rough idle, misfires, and performance issues. We run a full scan, explain what the codes actually mean, and only do what the car actually needs.
