What We Do

Our Services

Full-service shop handling all domestic and most import vehicles. We diagnose accurately, explain everything in plain terms, and fix it right the first time.

Wheel Alignment

Our specialty. Front-end and four-wheel alignment for cars, trucks, and SUVs. Fixes pulling, uneven tire wear, and steering drift. Most cars and small trucks start at $69.99.

Request this service →

Brake Repair

Pads, rotors, calipers, drums, and brake lines. We inspect the full system and only recommend what actually needs replacing. Squealing, grinding, or soft pedal — bring it in.

Request this service →

Steering & Suspension

Shocks, struts, tie rods, ball joints, and control arms. If your car pulls, floats, or clunks over bumps, we find the issue and tell you straight what it takes to fix it.

Request this service →

AC Repair

Diagnosis, recharge, compressor, and leak repair. Albuquerque summers are serious — we get your AC blowing cold before the heat hits. All makes and models.

Request this service →

Oil Changes

Conventional and synthetic using the spec for your vehicle. We check your fluids and flag anything worth your attention — no pressure to do more than you need.

Request this service →

Auto Electrical

Battery, alternator, starter, and wiring. Proper diagnostic tools to trace electrical faults accurately so you're not paying for guesswork.

Request this service →

Radiator & Cooling

Radiator repair, coolant flush, hoses, thermostat, and water pump. We've rescued road-trippers stranded right outside Albuquerque — we know cooling systems.

Request this service →

Engine Diagnostics

Check engine light, rough idle, misfires, performance issues. We run a full scan and explain what the codes actually mean before any work starts.

Request this service →

Tune-Ups & Maintenance

Spark plugs, filters, belts, and fluid services. We follow your manufacturer schedule and keep track of what's been done so nothing falls through the cracks.
